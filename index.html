Visiting Las Vegas, Zion National Park and Death Valley

Earlier this year in March, I decided to go on a trip with my best friend for my birthday. One of the places I had wanted to visit was Las Vegas after hearing about how fun and lively a city it was. We wanted to also visited a few other places while we were there so our Vegas trip ended up being more of a mini road trip around Nevada, Utah and Arizona. We decide to go for a week and visited the Las Vegas Strip, Zion National Park and Death Valley.

Las Vegas, NV

We wanted to try everything that we could in Vegas that we couldn't experience here in the midwest. When we landed in Vegas, we had to try in & out which was the first place we went to after picking up our rental car. We then headed to our hotel, The Signature at MGM Grand on the Las Vegas Strip. The drive to the hotel down the strip was incredible. The whole street was lit up and we got to see so many incredible places like the Cosmopolitan and the Bellagio Hotel.
